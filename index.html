Intentional Innovation & Equity

I am driven by the belief that technology must be used to tangibly improve the lives of Africans. Innovation should dismantle existing barriers, never reinforce them or exclude marginalised groups.


My technical foundation is anchored by a strong focus on data analysis using R and Python. More importantly, I synthesise these technical data insights to inform actionable national strategies. While my academic background specifically prepared me for the payments, economics, and financial sectors, my analytical interests span broadly across multiple developmental ecosystems.


Recently, my research has centred on the value proposition of stablecoins, culminating in the development of a targeted stability framework. Concurrently, as a Research Intern at Cenfri, I am deepening my exposure to developmental policy research, which is complemented by my global technical experience attending the 2025 Deep Dive into Blockchain summer school in Switzerland.
